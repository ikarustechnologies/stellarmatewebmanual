StellarMate App

You can connect to StellarMate Plus Controller in several ways, including:

  1. StellarMate Mobile App for Tablets.
  2. PC/Mac using KStars.
  3. Any Web browser or VNC client.


StellarMate App is the Official App for StellarMate gadget. It is available for both Android and iOS smartphones and tablets. It is primary designed for tablets in portrait mode. While it can be used for phones, for the best experience, use it with a tablet/iPad. Download and install the App on your device before you continue.
