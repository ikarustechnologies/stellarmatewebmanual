  1. StellarMate App

Setup

Go to your mobile OS WiFi settings and connect to the StellarMate hotspot.


Hotspot: stellarmate

Password: stellar@mate

IMPORTANT


Before using the StellarMate App to configure your unit for the first time, please use the networking settings in your iOS/Android phone/tablet to connect to the HotSpot first. Once connected, proceed to run the StellarMate App.



After connecting to StellarMate, go back to the App:


  • Tap on RESCAN to start the scanning process. Discovered devices would be listed on the left side pane.




  • After StellarMate is detected, tap on it to establish connection. Wait for a few seconds and the LED would turn green indicating a stable successful connection.



The right side pane should now display the Equipment Profile page. Now you are ready to connect to your astronomical equipment.
